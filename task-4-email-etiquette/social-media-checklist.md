## Part B – Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

| ✅ Do’s | ❌ Don’ts |
|--------|----------|
| Use strong and unique passwords | Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly |
| Think before posting any content | Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content |
| Maintain privacy settings | Do not click on suspicious links or messages |
| Share accurate and reliable information | Avoid engaging in online arguments or cyberbullying |
| Communicate respectfully and professionally | Do not overshare your location or daily activities |
